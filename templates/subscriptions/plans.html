Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade anytime?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Your new plan's validity starts from today.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept UPI, debit/credit cards, and net banking via our secure Razorpay gateway.

Is my data safe if I downgrade?

Yes. Data is never deleted on downgrade; you simply cannot add new records beyond your plan's limits.

Do plans auto-renew?

Not yet. You'll receive an email reminder 7 days before expiry and can renew manually.
